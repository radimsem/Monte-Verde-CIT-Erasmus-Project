What to do in Madeira

Explore Madeira's breathtaking landscapes by embarking on a thrilling levada walk. These narrow irrigation channels crisscross the island, offering scenic trails that wind through lush forests, picturesque valleys, and stunning coastal cliffs. Dive into the crystal-clear waters surrounding Madeira and discover a vibrant underwater world. Snorkeling and scuba diving enthusiasts can explore colorful coral reefs, encounter exotic marine life, and even dive alongside dolphins and turtles in their natural habitats. Take a scenic drive along Madeira's dramatic coastal roads, offering awe-inspiring views at every turn. From the dizzying heights of Cabo Girão, one of Europe's highest sea cliffs, to the rugged beauty of Ponta de São Lourenço, the island's easternmost point, the coastal scenery is a feast for the eyes.
